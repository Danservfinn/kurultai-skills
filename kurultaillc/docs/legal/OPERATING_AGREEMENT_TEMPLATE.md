# Kurultai LLC - Operating Agreement

> **Single-Member Limited Liability Company**
> **State**: Florida [Update if different]
> **Date**: [DATE OF FORMATION]

---

## Article 1: Formation

**1.1 Name.** The name of the Company is "Kurultai LLC" (the "Company").

**1.2 Organization.** The Company is formed as a Limited Liability Company under the laws of the State of Florida.

**1.3 Purpose.** The purpose of the Company is to engage in any lawful activity for which limited liability companies may be organized under the laws of the State of Florida.

---

## Article 2: Member

**2.1 Name and Address.** The name and address of the sole Member is:

[YOUR FULL NAME]
[YOUR STREET ADDRESS]
[YOUR CITY], [STATE] [ZIP CODE]

**2.2 Authority.** The Member has full and exclusive authority to manage and operate the Company and to make all decisions regarding the Company's business and affairs.

---

## Article 3: Capital Contributions

**3.1 Initial Contribution.** The Member shall make an initial capital contribution to the Company in the amount of $[AMOUNT], consisting of cash and/or property.

**3.2 Additional Contributions.** The Member may, but is not required to, make additional capital contributions to the Company.

---

## Article 4: Distributions

**4.1 Allocation of Profits and Losses.** All profits and losses of the Company shall be allocated to the Member.

**4.2 Distributions.** Distributions of cash or other assets of the Company shall be made to the Member at such times and in such amounts as the Member determines.

---

## Article 5: Management

**5.1 Member-Managed.** The Company shall be managed by the Member. The Member has full, exclusive, and complete authority to manage the Company.

**5.2 Authority.** The Member may act for and on behalf of the Company in all matters.

**5.3 Banking.** The Member is authorized to open bank accounts in the Company's name and to sign checks, drafts, and other instruments.

---

## Article 6: Meetings

**6.1 No Meetings Required.** No meetings of the Member are required.

---

## Article 7: Records

**7.1 Records.** The Company shall maintain at its principal office:

- A list of the full name and last known address of each Member
- A copy of the Articles of Organization and any amendments
- A copy of this Operating Agreement and any amendments
- Copies of the Company's federal, state, and local tax returns for the past 3 years
- Copies of any financial statements for the past 3 years

**7.2 Inspection.** The Member may inspect and copy any Company records during reasonable business hours.

---

## Article 8: Tax Matters

**8.1 Tax Treatment.** The Member shall report the Company's income, gains, losses, deductions, and credits on their federal income tax return.

**8.2 Tax Matters Partner.** The Member is designated as the "Tax Matters Partner" for purposes of the Internal Revenue Code.

---

## Article 9: Indemnification

**9.1 Indemnification.** The Company shall indemnify and hold harmless the Member from and against any and all claims, demands, actions, or losses brought against the Member arising out of the Company's business, except in cases of fraud, willful misconduct, or gross negligence.

---

## Article 10: Dissolution

**10.1 Events of Dissolution.** The Company shall dissolve upon:
- The written consent of the Member; or
- The entry of a decree of judicial dissolution

**10.2 Winding Up.** Upon dissolution, the Member shall wind up the Company's affairs and distribute the remaining assets to the Member.

---

## Article 11: Amendment

**11.1 Amendment.** This Operating Agreement may be amended, modified, or repealed only by the written consent of the Member.

---

## Article 12: Governing Law

**12.1 Governing Law.** This Operating Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Florida.

---

## Article 13: Severability

**13.1 Severability.** If any provision of this Operating Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

---

## Article 14: Entire Agreement

**14.1 Entire Agreement.** This Operating Agreement constitutes the entire agreement among the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof.

---

Revisit and amend if:
