## Phase 2: File Articles of Organization

### Florida Specific (Sunbiz)

- [ ] **File Articles of Organization** online:
  - Website: https://dos.myflorida.com/sunbiz/
  - Form: Articles of Organization (Form DR-2R105)
  - **Filing Fee**: $125 (one-time)
  - Processing: Same-day to 2 weeks

**Information Needed:**
- LLC name
- Registered agent name and address
- Principal office address
- Member(s) name and address
- Effective date (usually immediate)

### [ ] Obtain EIN (Employer Identification Number)

- [ ] **Apply online** (free, immediate):
  - Website: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
  - Required: SSN, legal name, business address
  - **Note**: Apply AFTER LLC is approved

## Phase 4: Ongoing Compliance

### Florida Requirements

- [ ] **Annual Report** (due January 1st each year)
  - File online: https://dos.myflorida.com/sunbiz/
  - Fee: $138.75
  - Late fee: $400 if missed

- [ ] **State Taxes**:
  - Florida: No state income tax âœ“
  - Florida LLC filing fee: None (only annual report)

### Federal Requirements

- [ ] **Tax Election** (Recommended: S-Corp)
  - File Form 2553 within 75 days of formation
  - Required if you want S-Corp tax treatment
  - **When to file**: After you have ~$60k+ annual profit

- [ ] **Annual Tax Return**:
  - Single-member LLC: Schedule C (sole prop) OR Form 1120-S (S-Corp)
  - Due: March 15 (S-Corp) or April 15 (sole prop)

## Phase 5: Optional But Recommended

### [ ] Operating Agreement

Even single-member LLCs should have one. It:
- Formalizes business structure
- Protects limited liability status
- Useful if you add members later

Template available: `/Users/kurultai/kurultai/kurultaillc/OPERATING_AGREAMENT_TEMPLATE.md`

### [ ] Business Address Privacy

- [ ] **Registered Agent Service** (~$100-200/year)
  - Keeps home address off public records
  - Accepts legal mail on your behalf
  - Recommended: Northwest, ZenBusiness, Bizee

- [ ] **Virtual Mailbox** (~$20-50/month)
  - Physical address for mail receipt
  - Digital scanning and forwarding
  - Examples: Earth Class Mail, Anytime Mailbox

### [ ] DUNS Number

- [ ] Apply for free: https://developer.dunsnumber.com/
- [ ] Builds business credit profile
- [ ] Required for some contracts
