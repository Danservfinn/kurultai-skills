# M&A Due Diligence Checklist

## Financial Due Diligence

### Revenue Quality
- [ ] Revenue recognition policy review
- [ ] Customer concentration analysis (top 10 customers %)
- [ ] Churn and retention metrics (last 12 quarters)
- [ ] Recurring vs. non-recurring revenue breakdown
- [ ] Revenue by product, geography, customer segment
- [ ] Backlog and pipeline analysis

### Expense Structure
- [ ] COGS components and trends
- [ ] Sales & marketing efficiency (CAC, LTV, payback period)
- [ ] R&D capitalization vs. expense policy
- [ ] G&A leverage analysis
- [ ] Outstanding obligations and commitments

### Balance Sheet
- [ ] Accounts receivable aging
- [ ] Accounts payable aging
- [ ] Debt instruments and covenants
- [ ] Off-balance sheet obligations
- [ ] Working capital trends and requirements
- [ ] Cash flow projection (24 months)

## Legal & Compliance

- [ ] Corporate structure and entity diagram
- [ ] Material contracts review (customer, vendor, partner)
- [ ] IP ownership and protection (patents, trademarks, copyrights)
- [ ] Litigation history and pending claims
- [ ] Regulatory compliance status
- [ ] Option grants and cap table accuracy
- [ ] Employee agreements and NDAs
- [ ] Open source software usage

## Product & Technology

- [ ] Technical architecture documentation
- [ ] Codebase assessment (quality, technical debt)
- [ ] Security audit results
- [ ] Scalability and performance benchmarks
- [ ] Product roadmap and dependencies
- [ ] Critical dependencies and single points of failure
- [ ] Technical team assessment (skills, retention risk)

## Commercial & Market

- [ ] Market size and growth rate (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- [ ] Competitive positioning matrix
- [ ] Customer satisfaction scores (NPS, CSAT)
- [ ] Win/loss analysis
- [ ] Pricing power and elasticity
- [ ] Channel partner analysis
- [ ] Brand strength assessment

## Organizational

- [ ] Org chart and role definitions
- [ ] Key person dependency analysis
- [ ] Retention risk assessment (top 20 performers)
- [ ] Cultural fit assessment
- [ ] Compensation benchmarking
- [ ] Benefit plans and obligations
- [ ] Union agreements or works council

## Red Flags

### Walk Away Immediately
- Fraud or intentional misrepresentation
- Unresolved IP ownership disputes
- Regulatory violations with material penalties
- Key customer >40% of revenue with contract expiring <12 months

### Price Adjustment or Structural Protection
- High customer concentration (top 3 >50%)
- Declining revenue trends (last 4 quarters)
- Key employee departures <6 months
- Pending litigation with material exposure
- Technical debt requiring rewrite

## Integration Planning

### Day 1
- [ ] Leadership communication plan
- [ ] Employee announcement script
- [ ] Customer and partner notifications
- [ ] Access rights setup (email, systems)
- [ ] Banking and finance integration

### First 90 Days
- [ ] Technology migration or integration plan
- [ ] Org rationalization and role mapping
- [ ] Culture assimilation activities
- [ ] Quick wins identification
- [ ] Synergy tracking dashboard
